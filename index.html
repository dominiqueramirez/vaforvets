Welcome to the VA's Veteran Employment Services Office (VESO) website! To get employment-related help in your area, visit this map. VESO’s VA for Vets career tools (i.e., Military Skills Translator, Resume Builder and Federal Job Search functions) are now accessible through the Veterans Employment Center (VEC) at eBenefits website. For more information about how VESO helps Veterans, read the VESO Fact Sheet.
Veteran Employment Center Tutorial Video

What is VA for Vets?

The Veteran Employment Services Office’s (VESO) VA for Vets initiative helps Veterans and transitioning military service members find meaningful careers. At VA for Vets , federal government human resources professionals and supervisors can find a multitude of resources to support Veteran hiring. Additionally, Veterans can check out job openings below or contact a Veteran Employment Specialist for more information on federal employment opportunities.

Deployment and Reintegration Services

Are you a National Guard or Reserve member who works at VA? You may benefit from the personalized deployment and reintegration services provided by VESO’s team of Regional Managers who offer free expert guidance to Military Service Members.


Learn More

Veterans' Careers at VA

VA is committed to hiring Veterans. If you are transitioning from the military, a Veteran already, an active Reserve or National Guard member, we invite you to explore the benefits of continuing your career at VA.
